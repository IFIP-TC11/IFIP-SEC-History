Venue

The 20th IFIP International Information Security Conference (IFIP/SEC 2005) will be held in International Conference Hall at Makuhari-Messe, Chiba, JAPAN.

Makuhari-Messe (access)

Presentation Equipments

All conference rooms will be equipped with computer and LCD projector. We also have wireless LAN available in all conference rooms. If you have your own laptop and it has wireless Internet capabilities, you will connect to Internet. No LAN card and no laptop computers will be provided for your Internet access. Computers in all conference rooms can support USB flash memory drive but cannot support floppy disk or CD-R. PowerPoint and PDF format file can be managed on the computers. Only 100V/50Hz AC electric power outlets are available in all conference rooms.
