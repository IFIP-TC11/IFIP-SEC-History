Accomodation

The venue of the conference is the NH Belfort Hotel in Ghent. Standing in the shadow of the Town Hall, the NH Gent Belfort hotel in Ghent waits for you to discover the riches it has to offer. The 174 room hotel maintains the ideal proximity to a wide range of architectural highlights and recreational delights for you to enjoy. At NH Gent Belfort, there are 10 functional meeting rooms, a convenient fitness center, and a private dining room.
