Venue

IFIP SEC 2015 and all co-located events will take place in the buildings of the ESA campus of University of Hamburg, which is located at Edmund-Siemers-Allee.

The venue is right in the heart of the city of Hamburg and in close walking distance (10 minutes) to beautiful Binnenalster, a water basin surrounded by parks that is very popular with locals and visitors alike.

The ESA campus is well-connected to public transport: Dammtor railway station, which is served by local as well as long-distance trains, is located right in front of the building (5 minutes walking distance). Hamburg Airport can be reached within 30 minutes by city trains (leaving every 10 minutes).
