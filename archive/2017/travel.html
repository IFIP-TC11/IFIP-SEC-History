Travel Information

The conference will be hosted at the National Research Council of Italy (CNR) that is located in Rome, Piazzale Aldo Moro 7 (zipcode 00185, Rome, Italy), in the city centre of Rome, and near the main train station (Termini). The place is in front of the main University in Rome (“La Sapienza”). Rome is centrally located in Italy, easily accessible by air, by train and by car.

Arriving from Fiumicino airport:

  Itinerary Timetable
  Outside Fiumicino airport   
reach coach station Fiumicino Air Terminal (FS Trenitalia)  
take bus line FM1 (Settebagni)  - 8 stops FM1 every15 min.
get off at  Tiburtina station  
then reach  Staz. Tiburtina/Metrò B   
take bus line 71 (S. Silvestro) - 6 stops every10 min. 
get off at  Via dei Marrucini  
by foot 100 metres backwards  
thus arriving in  Piazzale Aldo Moro  7 (main entrance)  
or, by bus, take line 492 (P.zza Risorgimento) - 6 stops every 7 min. 
get off at  Via dei Ramni  
by foot 10 metres backwards  
thus arriving in  Via dei Ramni, 19 (secondary entrance)  
Please note:    

'Leonardo Express' is the non-stop train service connecting Termini Station, Rome’s train hub, with Fiumicino airport in only 32 minutes, with trains departing every 15 minutes (during certain times of the day service may pass to one train every 30 minutes). The Termini Station can also be reached by bus from the bus station outside Fiumicino airport. Cnr headquarters are about 1,5 Km from Termini Station (bus 492, or by foot following the itinerary indicated in this map).
